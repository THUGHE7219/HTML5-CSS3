About

**FROM THE DRAGOART WEBSITE**


Dawn is an introverted, intuitive, eccentric, laid back artist that's been drawing since she was 9. She started to get serious with drawing around the age of 13. She loves to expand her knowledge and experiment with new things/styles. Her style in art varies between her spurts of inspiration.

Dawn's Hobbies

Drawing, reading, writing, working, and creating characters. She is very interested on writing/illustrating stories as well as effective character creation. Always on the look out of improving herself on various levels. She finds interest in macabre, concept, anime, creatures, and movies. Maybe someday She will become a director; it's the only available option to get these writhing stems of creativity born into the world.
